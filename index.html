01 — Profile

About Me

Where clinical training meets commerce — a short read on how a pharmacy diploma turned into a portfolio of digital health ventures.

"I treat a product page the way I was trained to treat a prescription — precise, checked twice, and built to help the person on the other end."

I'm Bijaya Bhandari, a Kathmandu-based pharmacist and e-commerce SEO strategist. I started in clinical pharmacy, earning my Diploma in Pharmacy in 2014, and have since built a career bridging healthcare and digital commerce — writing and optimizing product content for online pharmacies, and shaping SEO strategy that helps people in every district of Nepal find the medicines and health products they need.

Alongside pharmacy and digital marketing, I've trained in NEPSE technical analysis (2024) to read Nepal's stock market with the same discipline I bring to a dosage chart, and completed dedicated AI training (2025) to bring automation and smarter workflows into the businesses I help run.

Today my work spans Daju Bhai Pharmacy, Pharmamandu Dot Com Pvt. Ltd., Civil Medicare Center, and Unigrow — a small but growing group of ventures built around one idea: healthcare in Nepal should be easy to find, trust, and access online.
