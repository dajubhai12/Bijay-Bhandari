About

From the dispensing counter to the boardroom

Bijaya Bhandari trained as a pharmacist at Farwest School of Medicine, Bhimdatta, Kanchanpur, completing a Diploma in Pharmacy in 2071 B.S. That grounding in dispensing and patient care now sits alongside a growing group of healthcare and distribution ventures across Nepal, with a particular focus on oncology care and medicine access.

Beyond the pharmacy counter, Bijaya has trained as a NEPSE technical analyst and completed AI training in 2082 B.S., and now mentors others in applying AI tools — bringing a cross-disciplinary approach to healthcare, markets and technology.
